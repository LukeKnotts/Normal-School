Students

We take the welfare of our students very seriously at Hippocampus High School. We hope to provide a fun and safe environment where education and friendships thrive. Since we serve a great number of kids at Hippocampus High School, it is important to us that we keep things organized and safe. At Hippocampus High School, the different grades are divided into colors. This helps you, as a family, know exactly where your child should go and what they need at different points. The list is as follows:

  • Freshmans
  • Sophmores
  • an alternative Sophmore color
  • Juniors
  • Seniors
  • Super Seniors*
  • Alumni

*Hippocampus High School offers classes for a potential fifth year of high school for students who qualify and are trying to take certain classes under the high school. Whatever position your family is in, we have your back.
